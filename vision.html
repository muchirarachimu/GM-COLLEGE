WHO WE ARE

GM OPTIPEX TECHNICAL COLLEGE is a private Kenyan technical University. Our main objective is to bridge the gap in ensuring that the youths are fully equipped with professional skills in science and Technology particularly in computing and informatics so that they are able to be self reliant in a sustainable manner as well as contributing to the social and economic development of the country. The university was founded in 2015 and is accredited by The Commission for University Education (CUE).
